# International shipping, customs, and duties

We ship to over 90 countries. Costs, timing, and customs vary by destination.

## Where we ship

You can see the full list at checkout — set your country from the country picker. If your country isn't listed, we don't ship there yet. Sign up for our newsletter to be notified when we add new destinations.

## Delivery times

| Region | Standard | Express |
|--------|----------|---------|
| Canada / Mexico | 5–10 business days | 2–4 |
| Europe / UK | 7–14 | 3–6 |
| Asia / Pacific | 10–18 | 4–7 |
| Latin America | 10–21 | 5–10 |
| Africa / Middle East | 14–28 | 7–14 |

Times are estimates after the package ships. Customs delays can add several days.

## Shipping cost

Rates depend on weight, destination, and method. The exact cost appears at checkout before you pay. Orders over **$150 USD** often qualify for free standard shipping.

## Customs, duties, and taxes

For most countries, **duties and taxes are not included** in the price at checkout. You'll pay the carrier or your local customs office before delivery.

We offer **DDP (delivered duty paid)** for select countries — duties are added to your total at checkout and there's nothing more to pay on delivery. Look for the **All-inclusive pricing** banner at checkout.

You're responsible for any customs paperwork your country requires. If a package is rejected at customs or unclaimed, it's returned to us — we'll refund the order minus return shipping.

## Restricted items

Some products can't ship internationally due to regulations: aerosols, lithium batteries (in some quantities), liquids over 100ml on certain routes. Restricted items are marked on the product page.

## Returns from outside the US

International returns are accepted within 45 days. Customers pay return shipping; duties are non-refundable.
