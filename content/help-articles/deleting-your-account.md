# Deleting your account

You can permanently delete your account and personal data anytime. This is irreversible.

## Before you delete

Make sure you've:

- Cancelled any active subscriptions (deleting doesn't auto-cancel — see [Cancelling a subscription](cancelling-a-subscription.md)).
- Used or transferred any gift card balance — it's forfeited on deletion.
- Resolved any open returns or refunds.
- Downloaded any data you want to keep (**Account → Privacy → Download my data**).

## How to delete

1. Sign in and go to **Account → Privacy → Delete account**.
2. Read the disclosure and confirm with your password.
3. We send a confirmation email with a final cancel link (active for **24 hours**).
4. Click the link if you change your mind. Otherwise, deletion begins automatically after 24 hours.

## What gets deleted

- Profile, addresses, payment methods, sign-in credentials
- Order history (after legal retention period — see below)
- Subscription data, preferences, saved items
- Reviews and comments you've posted (your name is replaced with "Deleted user"; content stays unless you remove it first)

## What we have to keep

By law, we retain certain records for **7 years** after deletion:

- Order and invoice records (tax compliance)
- Records of payments and refunds (fraud and accounting)

This data is access-restricted and used only for legal compliance.

## Can I undo it?

You have **24 hours** after submitting the deletion to cancel via the email link. After that, the data is purged and we can't recover it.

If you want to use our service again later, you'll create a new account from scratch.

## GDPR and regional rights

If you're in the EU, UK, California, or other regions with data protection laws, you can also:

- Request a copy of all data we hold on you.
- Request correction of inaccurate data.
- Object to specific uses (e.g., marketing).

Visit **Account → Privacy** or email our data protection team at privacy@example.com.
