# Pausing a subscription

If you need a break but don't want to cancel, you can pause for **1, 2, or 3 months**. We hold your plan and pricing — billing resumes automatically when the pause ends.

## How to pause

1. Sign in and go to **Account → Subscriptions**.
2. Click the subscription, then **Pause subscription**.
3. Choose how long: 1, 2, or 3 months.
4. Confirm. We'll email a reminder before billing resumes.

## What happens during a pause

- **Recurring billing:** paused. No charges during the pause window.
- **Access to features:** depends on the plan.
  - **Product subscriptions** (boxes, refills): shipments are skipped.
  - **Service subscriptions** (software, content): access continues at your current plan level.
- **Pricing:** locked at your current rate. Even if prices go up, you keep your old price when billing resumes.

## Resume early

You can end a pause anytime. Open **Account → Subscriptions**, click the paused subscription, and choose **Resume now**. Your next billing date shifts to today.

## Extend a pause

You can extend up to the 3-month maximum. If you reach the 3-month limit and still need more time, the subscription auto-cancels — but you keep access until the end of the original billing period and can re-subscribe anytime.

## How often can I pause?

Once per 12-month period for free. Additional pauses are available on annual plans only.

## Pause vs cancel

| | Pause | Cancel |
|---|-------|--------|
| Keep current pricing | Yes | No |
| Skip billing | Yes | Yes |
| Keep history | Yes | Yes (90 days) |
| Auto-resume | Yes | No |

If you're sure you want to leave, see [Cancelling a subscription](cancelling-a-subscription.md).
