# Updating your account email

You can change the email address tied to your account at any time. Your order history, subscriptions, and saved preferences are preserved.

## Change your email

1. Sign in and go to **Account → Profile**.
2. Click **Edit** next to your email address.
3. Enter the new email and your current password.
4. We send a verification link to the new address (expires in **24 hours**).
5. Click the link to confirm. Until you confirm, the old email stays active.

## What changes

- All notifications (order updates, billing, marketing) go to the new email.
- Sign-in uses the new email.
- Past order receipts already sent are not re-sent — keep them for your records.

## What stays the same

- Order history, subscriptions, addresses, payment methods, loyalty points.
- Account creation date and tenure.

## Already lost access to your old email?

If you can't access the old inbox to receive the security notice, contact support with:

- Your account's name and a recent order number.
- The old email and the new one you'd like to use.
- A way for us to verify identity (recent purchase amount, billing ZIP, etc.).

We verify manually and update within 2 business days.

## Sign in with social provider

If your account uses Sign in with Google, Apple, or Facebook, your email is managed by that provider. Change it there, and your account here updates automatically the next time you sign in.
