# Tracking your shipment

Every order with a physical item gets a tracking number once it ships. You can track the package directly from your account or with the carrier.

## Find your tracking number

1. Sign in and go to **Orders**.
2. Click the order you want to track.
3. The tracking number and carrier appear under **Shipment details**. Click **Track package** to open the carrier's tracker.

You'll also receive a shipping confirmation email with the same tracking link.

## Tracking statuses

- **Label created** — we've printed the shipping label but the carrier hasn't picked up the package yet. This can take up to 24 hours.
- **In transit** — the package is moving through the carrier's network.
- **Out for delivery** — the package is on the truck for delivery today.
- **Delivered** — the carrier marked the package as delivered.
- **Exception** — something interrupted delivery (wrong address, weather, damage). Check the carrier site for details.

## Tracking hasn't updated

Tracking can pause for 24–72 hours during long transit legs (especially international shipments or weekends). If there's no update for more than **5 business days**, contact us and we'll open an investigation with the carrier.

## Package marked delivered but not received

1. Check around your property — porches, side gates, with neighbors, and at your mail room.
2. Wait 24 hours — carriers sometimes mark packages delivered a day early.
3. If it still hasn't arrived, contact support within **7 days of the delivery scan** so we can file a claim with the carrier.
