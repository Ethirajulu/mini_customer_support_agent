# Damaged or missing items

We're sorry — let's make it right. The fix depends on what went wrong.

## Report the problem

You have **14 days from delivery** to report a damaged or missing item.

1. Sign in and go to **Orders**.
2. Open the order and click **Report a problem**.
3. Choose the issue: damaged, missing, or wrong item.
4. Upload photos (required for damaged items).
5. Tell us what you'd like: replacement, refund, or store credit.

We respond within 1 business day, usually with an instant resolution.

## Damaged item

If an item arrived broken, scratched, or unusable:

- Take photos of the damaged item **and** the outside of the box.
- Don't throw away the packaging until we confirm next steps — the carrier may need to inspect it.
- We typically ship a replacement at no charge, no need to return the broken one.

## Missing item from a multi-item order

Sometimes items in the same order ship in separate boxes. Check the order page — if you see **multiple shipments**, the missing item may still be on the way.

If everything shows as delivered but an item isn't in the box:

- Look inside the packaging — small items can be tucked in air pillows or paper.
- Check the packing slip against your order to confirm what should be inside.
- Report the missing item with a photo of the packing slip.

## Wrong item received

Keep the wrong item until we send you a return label (free). Once we scan the return, we'll ship the correct item.

## Item missing parts

For items with assembly (furniture, electronics), report the specific missing parts. The manufacturer can often ship a replacement part directly to you faster than a full replacement.
