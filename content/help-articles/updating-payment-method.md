# Updating your payment method

Keep your payment method current to avoid interrupted service or failed orders.

## Add or update a card

1. Sign in and go to **Account → Payment methods**.
2. Click **Add payment method** or select an existing card and click **Edit**.
3. Enter card details and click **Save**. We run a small verification charge (immediately voided).

We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and PayPal.

## Set a default

Mark any payment method as default. Subscriptions and one-tap purchases use your default automatically.

## Failed payment

If a recurring charge fails:

1. We retry automatically over the next 7 days.
2. You'll receive email reminders to update your card.
3. After 7 days of failed retries, the subscription is paused until you update.

You can fix this immediately by adding a new payment method and clicking **Retry now** on the failed invoice.

## Removing a card

You can remove any card that isn't your default. To remove a default card, add a new one first or set another card as default.

## Security

We never store full card numbers on our servers. Payment details are tokenized through our PCI-compliant payment processor.
