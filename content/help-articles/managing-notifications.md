# Managing your notifications

Control which emails, texts, and push notifications you receive.

## Where to manage preferences

1. Sign in and go to **Account → Notifications**.
2. Toggle each category on or off.
3. Save changes.

Some notifications are mandatory (security alerts, order confirmations, billing receipts) and can't be turned off.

## Notification categories

- **Order updates** — confirmations, shipping, delivery. Mandatory.
- **Security alerts** — sign-ins from new devices, password changes. Mandatory.
- **Account & billing** — receipts, renewal reminders, failed payments. Mandatory.
- **Marketing & promotions** — sales, new arrivals, restock alerts. Optional.
- **Product recommendations** — personalized picks based on your activity. Optional.
- **Surveys & feedback** — occasional requests for your input. Optional.
- **Loyalty program** — points balance, reward notifications. Optional.

## Channel preferences

For each optional category, choose where you want updates:

- Email
- SMS (carrier rates may apply)
- Push (mobile app)
- In-app only

## Unsubscribe from email quickly

Every marketing email has an **unsubscribe** link in the footer. Clicking it removes you from that specific list immediately. Transactional emails (orders, security) don't have an unsubscribe link by design.

## SMS

Reply **STOP** to any text to opt out of all SMS. Reply **HELP** for support contact info. Standard message and data rates apply.

## Push notifications (mobile app)

Manage these in your device settings:

- **iOS:** Settings → Notifications → [App name]
- **Android:** Settings → Apps → [App name] → Notifications

You can also disable them inside the app under **Account → Notifications**.

## Why am I still getting emails after unsubscribing?

It can take up to 48 hours for unsubscribe to propagate across our systems. If you still receive marketing emails after that, contact support with the subject line of the email.
