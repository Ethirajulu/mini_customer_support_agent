# Returning an item

Returns are accepted within **30 days of delivery**. You can return for a refund, exchange, or store credit.

## Start a return

1. Sign in and go to **Orders**.
2. Open the order and click **Return items**.
3. Select the items, the reason, and your preferred resolution (refund, exchange, or store credit).
4. We'll email a prepaid return label and instructions.

## Packaging your return

- Use the original packaging if possible, or any sturdy box.
- Include the items in original condition with tags attached.
- Place the return label on the outside; remove any old shipping labels.

## Drop-off

Drop the package at any carrier location listed on your label. We recommend keeping the tracking receipt until the refund is processed.

## Return shipping costs

- **Free returns:** defective items, wrong item shipped, damaged in transit.
- **Customer-paid returns:** change of mind, wrong size ordered. We deduct **$6.95** from your refund for the prepaid label, or you can ship at your own cost.

## Exchanges

For exchanges (e.g., different size), we ship the replacement as soon as the carrier scans your return — you don't have to wait for it to arrive at our warehouse.

## After we receive your return

- Inspection takes 2–3 business days.
- Refunds are issued to the original payment method (see [How to refund an order](refunding-an-order.md) for timing).
- If the item arrives damaged, used, or with missing parts, we may reduce the refund or return the item to you.

## Items that can't be returned

- Final-sale items (clearly marked at checkout)
- Personalized or custom-made items
- Opened intimate apparel, swimwear without liner intact
- Perishables, gift cards, and digital downloads
