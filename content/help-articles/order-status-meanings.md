# What do the order statuses mean?

When you open an order, you'll see one of the following statuses. Here's what each one means and how long it usually takes.

## Pending

We've received your order but haven't charged your payment method yet. This usually clears within a few minutes.

**What to expect:** the status moves to **Processing** automatically.

## Processing

Payment is confirmed and your order is queued for fulfillment.

**Typical duration:** 1–2 business days.
**Can I make changes?** Yes — you can still edit the address or cancel before fulfillment begins.

## Awaiting fulfillment

The warehouse is picking and packing your items.

**Typical duration:** under 24 hours.
**Can I make changes?** Only by contacting support, and only if the pack-out hasn't started yet.

## Shipped

The package has left our warehouse. You'll see a tracking number on the order page.

**Typical duration to delivery:** 2–7 business days domestic, 7–21 international.

## Out for delivery

The carrier is delivering the package today.

## Delivered

The carrier confirmed delivery. If you didn't receive it, see [Tracking your shipment](tracking-your-shipment.md).

## On hold

Something needs attention before we can ship — usually a payment issue, address verification, or stock check. We'll email you with the next step.

## Cancelled

The order was cancelled by you, by support, or automatically (e.g., payment failed and couldn't be recovered). Any charges are refunded.

## Returned

The order was returned to us by the carrier (usually undeliverable). We'll contact you to re-ship or refund.

## Refunded

Money has been issued back to your original payment method. See [How to refund an order](refunding-an-order.md) for timing.
