# Changing your subscription plan

You can upgrade, downgrade, or switch between monthly and annual billing anytime.

## How to change plans

1. Sign in and go to **Account → Subscriptions**.
2. Click your subscription and select **Change plan**.
3. Pick a new plan and confirm.

## Upgrading

Upgrades take effect **immediately**. You get the new features right away.

**Billing:** we prorate. You pay the price difference for the remaining time in the current billing period. Example: if you upgrade halfway through a $20 month to a $40 plan, you're charged $10 now and the next renewal is at $40.

## Downgrading

Downgrades take effect at the **end of your current billing period**. You keep your current features and pricing until then.

We don't refund the difference for the current period.

## Switching between monthly and annual

- **Monthly → Annual:** takes effect immediately. We charge the annual price (minus prorated credit for unused monthly time) and start a new annual cycle today.
- **Annual → Monthly:** takes effect when your annual term ends. We don't refund unused annual time.

## Plan comparison

You can compare features side-by-side on the **Pricing** page or in the in-app plan picker. If you're not sure which plan fits your usage, our support team can recommend one — share your typical usage and we'll suggest the best match.

## Failed upgrade

If the upgrade payment fails:

1. We retry the charge over 3 days.
2. Your plan stays at the current level (no partial upgrade).
3. Update your payment method to retry. See [Updating your payment method](updating-payment-method.md).

## Grandfathered pricing

If you're on a legacy plan we no longer offer, you can stay on it as long as you don't switch. Once you change plans, you can't go back to the old one.
