# Cancelling a subscription

You can cancel anytime. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period — you keep access until then and aren't charged again.

## How to cancel

1. Sign in and open **Account → Subscriptions**.
2. Click the subscription you want to cancel.
3. Click **Cancel subscription** and confirm.

You'll get an email confirming the cancellation and the date access ends.

## What happens to my data?

- **Active period (until billing end date):** full access, no changes.
- **After cancellation:** account moves to a read-only state. You can still sign in, view past orders, and export data for **90 days**.
- **After 90 days:** data is deleted unless you reactivate.

## Refunds for unused time

Subscriptions are non-refundable for the current billing period, but:

- **Annual plans** cancelled within **14 days** of renewal are eligible for a prorated refund.
- **Monthly plans** are not eligible for partial refunds.

## Pause instead of cancelling

If you're not sure you want to leave, you can pause for up to 3 months. See [Pausing a subscription](pausing-a-subscription.md).

## Can't find the cancel button?

If you subscribed through the iOS or Android app, you must cancel through Apple's App Store or Google Play. We can't cancel app-store subscriptions on your behalf.
