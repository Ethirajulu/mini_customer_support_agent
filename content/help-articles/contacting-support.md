# Contacting customer support

We're happy to help. Pick the channel that fits your issue.

## Channels

| Channel | Best for | Hours | Typical response |
|---------|----------|-------|------------------|
| Live chat | Quick questions, order status | 24/7 | Under 2 minutes |
| Email | Detailed issues, attachments | Always open | Within 24 hours |
| Phone | Urgent or sensitive issues | Mon–Fri, 8am–8pm ET | Wait under 5 min |
| Social DM | General questions | Mon–Sun, 9am–6pm ET | Within a few hours |

- **Chat:** click the **Help** button in the bottom-right of any page.
- **Email:** support@example.com
- **Phone:** 1-800-555-0199 (US/Canada). International numbers are listed at **Help → Contact**.

## Help us help you faster

Include in your first message:

- Your **order number** (starts with `ORD-`) or account email
- A **brief description** of what's wrong
- **Screenshots** if there's a visual issue
- **What you've already tried**

This usually cuts response time in half.

## Self-service first

Before reaching out, try:

- **Help Center** — searchable articles on the most common questions.
- **Order page** — refunds, returns, address changes, and cancellations can be done yourself.
- **Status page** at status.example.com — check if there's an ongoing outage.

## Escalations

If you've been working with us and feel stuck:

- Reply to your support thread with the words "**escalate**" — it routes to a senior agent.
- Ask for a callback if email back-and-forth isn't working.

## Feedback (not a support issue)

For feature requests, suggestions, or general feedback, email feedback@example.com. We read every message, but can't always reply individually.
