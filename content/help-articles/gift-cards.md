# Buying and redeeming gift cards

Gift cards are available in $25, $50, $100, $250, and custom amounts up to $1,000.

## Buying a gift card

1. Go to **Shop → Gift Cards**.
2. Choose an amount and a delivery method:
   - **Email** — sent immediately or scheduled for a future date.
   - **Physical card** — mailed in a gift envelope (3–5 business days).
3. Personalize with a recipient name and message.
4. Pay with any accepted method **except** another gift card.

You'll get an order confirmation, and the recipient gets a separate email or package.

## Redeeming a gift card

1. Add items to your cart and go to **Checkout**.
2. Find the **Gift card** field and enter the code (16 characters).
3. The balance is applied to your order. Any remainder stays on the card for next time.

You can use multiple gift cards on one order. Combine with promo codes too.

## Check your balance

- **Online:** sign in and open **Account → Gift cards**.
- **By code:** visit **Gift card balance** (no sign-in needed) and enter the code.

## Expiration

Gift cards never expire. No fees, no monthly deductions.

## Lost or stolen cards

- **Email gift cards:** we can resend to the original email or transfer the unused balance to a new code. Have the purchase order number ready.
- **Physical cards:** we can replace stolen cards if you have the purchase order number and the card hasn't been redeemed.

We can't replace cards bought from third-party retailers — contact that retailer directly.

## Refunds with gift cards

If you paid with a gift card and need a refund, the refund returns to the same gift card (the balance is restored).
