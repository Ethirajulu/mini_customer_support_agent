# How to refund an order

You can request a refund for any order within **30 days of delivery**. Refunds are issued to the original payment method.

## Eligibility

- The order was placed within the last 30 days.
- The item is unused, in its original packaging, and in resalable condition.
- Final-sale, personalized, and perishable items are **not** refundable.

## How to request a refund

1. Sign in to your account and go to **Orders**.
2. Find the order and click **Request refund**.
3. Select the items you want refunded and choose a reason.
4. Choose a refund method: original payment method or store credit (store credit is processed instantly).
5. Submit the request.

You'll receive a confirmation email once the refund is approved, typically within 1 business day.

## When will I see the money?

| Payment method | Time to appear |
|----------------|----------------|
| Credit / debit card | 5–10 business days |
| PayPal | 1–3 business days |
| Store credit | Instant |
| Bank transfer | 7–14 business days |

## Partial refunds

If only part of your order was wrong or damaged, you can request a partial refund for those items only. Shipping fees are refunded only if the entire order is returned or the issue was caused by us.

## Refund denied?

If your refund was denied and you believe it was in error, reply to the denial email or contact support. We review every appeal within 2 business days.
