# Resetting your password

If you've forgotten your password or want to change it, you can reset it in a few steps.

## Reset a forgotten password

1. Go to the sign-in page and click **Forgot password?**
2. Enter the email address on your account.
3. Check your inbox for a reset link (it expires in **60 minutes**).
4. Click the link, choose a new password, and sign in.

If you don't see the email within 5 minutes, check your spam folder and search for the sender domain.

## Change a password you remember

1. Sign in and open **Account → Security**.
2. Click **Change password**.
3. Enter your current password, then your new one twice.

## Password requirements

- At least 12 characters
- Mix of upper and lower case
- At least one number or symbol
- Cannot reuse your last 5 passwords

## Didn't receive the reset email?

- Confirm you're using the email tied to your account. Try other addresses you may have signed up with.
- Whitelist our domain in your email client.
- If you signed in with Google, Apple, or another social provider, you don't have a password — reset it through that provider instead.

If none of these work, contact support with the email and approximate date you created the account.
